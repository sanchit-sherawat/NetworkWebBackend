Dr. LaSheika Hill is a retired military leader with over 20 years of honorable service and more than two decades of experience in human resources and leadership development. She is a Licensed Qualified Mental Health Professional (QMHP) with extensive expertise in clinical, pastoral, and Christian-based counseling.

Dr. Hill holds multiple advanced degrees in theology, counseling, and strategic leadership, and is currently pursuing further credentials in clinical mental health and interdisciplinary exercise science.

A certified John Maxwell Leadership Coach, Trainer, and Speaker, she is also certified in DISC personality training and youth leadership.

Dr. Hill has delivered thousands of hours of therapy and coaching, specializing in trauma-informed care, addiction recovery, marriage and family counseling, and adolescent development. Her dynamic leadership has revitalized educational programs, improved client outcomes, and empowered individuals to overcome personal and professional barriers.

Through mentorship, faith-based guidance, and strategic insight, she continues to help others thrive emotionally, spiritually, and purposefully.
