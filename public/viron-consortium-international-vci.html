VIRON Consortium International (“VCI”)

The Civic Government of VIRON.NETWORK

“For The People – By The People”, VIRON Network is Governed by the Voices & Votes of The VIRON Consortium International (“VCI”), which is comprised of international partners with a civic purpose. Collectively, the men and women of The VCI have supported VIRON Network’s base and are reinforcing its backbone so that the civic objectives, missions, and vision of The VCI can be a reality to the many individuals that are in dire need.

The VCI has delegated and have chosen VIRON NETWORK ADVISOR to drive forward the new evolution of the Network Marketing industry.
