Our Mission

THE PURPOSE & ROLE OF VIRON

VIRON’s purpose is civic. VIRON’s "role" in your life is to essentially be your “Unprecedented-Upline" for you and for your entire Downline.

Emphasis, in the Network Marketing industry, we have all heard the statement "your Upline will help you". Well, naturally, most people want to help others, but there is no existing structure in place to help people efficiently. It is quite arbitrary out there nowadays.

For example, if your Upline sponsors a "John Doe", that action takes AWAY from your market. Meaning, that "John" can no longer be a part of your network/downline, nor will that "John" benefit you per any commissions, etc.

And understand that most likely a "spill" into your downline will mean little. A "spill" in that scenario really means nothing or a facade of value (especially if the MLM program has certain "qualification" requirements that you have not even met).

Further, when the MLM company reports that they are "exploding" in a certain country or market...I am here to tell you that it is NOT a good thing for you! It simply means that YOUR potential market is now absorbed by the company! So, WHY ARE YOU SO EXCITED ABOUT THAT?? WHAT ARE YOU CELEBRATING FOR?? VIRON wants to help you stop being “mislead" by false hype and facades!

With VIRON, the situation is completely different. When we sponsor "John Doe", we can RE-ASSIGN John to be under YOUR account (as if you sponsored "John" yourself)!

So, a "SPILL" in this nature is of the highest value because YOU get all the applicable Commissions, Bonuses, Residuals, etc. from that action/result!

Again, VIRON can help you because our systems and technologies enable us to do so.

SUMMARY OF VIRON’s PURPOSE & ROLE.

EMPOWERING YOU & YOUR NETWORK:

VIRON will be your Unprecedented Support Center to empower your Network Marketing Business. The third-party MLM company has been integrated with VIRON. *NOTE: The qualified MLM company’s details are located in the VIRON Member’s back-office.

With VIRON to professionally power your MLM business, you can earn an income from home without needing to Sell or Recruit anyone.

After registering your account at VIRON.NETWORK, you will be prompted to take a position with the MLM company. When that step is complete, your VIRON Business will be complete.

YOUR SYSTEMATIC NETWORK:

Once your VIRON account and your SC account set-up is complete, your VIRON position will be the same as your SC position in the network. Importantly, your Direct Referrals (“DRs”) in your VIRON.NETWORK will follow you into your SC downline accordingly.

YOUR PERMANENT NETWORK:

VIRON will generate (DRs) and/or close DRs (on your behalf) … and then place said DRs into your account. This action will grow your network.

*If for any reason one of your DRs in your Network becomes inactive or cause a “hole” in your downline, VIRON can replace or "patch up" that inactive/hole with a new and active/paying DR! *Further details on this are provided in the members back-office.

YOUR STABLE NETWORK:

Traditionally, when a third-party MLM company (aka: “VEHICLE”) gets compromised or worse goes out of business, you would lose your entire network/downline with it. Then you will have to start all over!

However, not with VIRON.NETWORK because within our diversified business model, there will be multiple VEHICLEs’ strategically integrated with VIRON. So, if any one VEHICLE gets compromised or goes out of business, we simply "SWAP" that with the next VEHICLE that is waiting in line. There are many 5-Star rated MLM companies out there (VEHICLEs are considered “a dime a dozen”). A very good thing.
