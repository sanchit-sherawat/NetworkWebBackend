Reverend Abraham Yew

Reverend Abraham Yew
Managing Partner & Chief Accountant

Visionary ~ Business Development ~ Team Training & Management.

An innovative and forward-thinking sales/operations director with 30+ years of extensive sales management, coaching leadership, financial & real estate consulting, and training experience within highly competitive markets.

Analytical strategist skilled in successfully analyzing investment opportunities, maximizing team performance through strategic training techniques, and increasing profits through careful organizational management.

Rev Abraham Yew, a Pastor with a mission calling and promised commission: "Blessed to be a blessing to the nations".

Worked in the fields of teaching, accounting, and auditing in one of the big fours, owned a partnership business in home interiors decoration and turn-key contract projects, before being called by God to the pastoral ministry.

Served 21 years in many capacities within the institutional church, on various boards such as the Board of Finance, overseeing churches as a District Superintendent and served at the national level.

I believe Jesus Christ as Savior and Lord and that this faith must also have reality in the here and now expressions. Thus, started my journey online to find and create that reality since 1998.

Today I serve as the President of my ministry called SOKOGIM - acronym for "Sons Of the Kingdom of God International Movement".

Pastor Yew truly feels that VIRON.NETWORK is the reality vehicle God will use powerfully and I am on it with others believing that together we will make a big difference and to civically impact our world.
